The Bund

The Bund, may be a common sight for people who live in Shanghai, is one of the first places for tourists to visit when entering the city.
When visiting the Bund, tourists will be able to see the highest skyscrapers in the city.

Shanghai Disney

The Shanghai Disney has been attracting Disney lovers from all over the world since 2016.
The park has seven themed areas and is located in Pudong, Shanghai.

Oriental Pearl Tower

Shanghai Oriental Pearl Tower is located in Lujiazui, Pudong New Area.
The tower is about 468 meters high and is the world's sixth tallest TV tower.
Visitors can travel up and down the Oriental Pearl Tower in double-decker elevators that provides introduction of the tower in both English and Chinese.

Tianzifang

Tianzifang is known for trendy art studios, small stores, coffee shops and narrow alleys.
It is one of the most popular tourist destination in Shanghai since it is a great place for shopping and taking photos.
