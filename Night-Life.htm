How is Shanghai's nightlife?

With a huge number of travellers, business-people and migrants, it is safe to say Shanghai's nightlife caters to a varied crowd.
Whether you want to spend your night at a quiet bar or a high-end, crowded club, there are more than enough
options to find the right place for you in Shanghai at night.
So here are some of the most popular bars and clubs in Shanghai...

TAXX, Shanghai

TAXX is one of the biggest nightclubs in Shanghai. This place is packed almost every day with EDM lovers.
TAXX does not have a fixed theme for what kind of music they are going to play, but it will mostly be EDM and Hip Pop.
The club hosts Top 100 DJ events monthly to attract the crowds.

Xintiandi Street

This 500-meter-long road in Xintiandi is one of the best places for people to spend their night life in Shanghai.
Both tourists and residents can shop, watch performances, take photos or dine out during the operation times.
The area is composed of old-style Shijumen residences and modern architecture,
which provides you the taste of both Shanghai in the 1920s and the modern lifestyle of urbanites of the 21st century.

The Monkey Lounge

This venue is known for hip hop, which makes it unique from all the other mega clubs.
The venue serves a wide array of champagnes and whiskies, which highly attracts international tourists.
